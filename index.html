Safety First! Always obey the rules of the road. Obey all traffic signals, signs, and laws. Get in the mindset of “driving” your bike—not just “riding” your bike. This will help you be a more focused and legally compliant bike rider.

The thin shells on most helmets now tend to hide any dents in the foam. But if you can see marks on the shell or measure any foam crush at all, replace the helmet.

Honda CBR150R Highlights:

The 2021 Honda CBR150R has been launched in Indonesia. The updated model comes with a much more aggressive bodywork similar to the Honda CBR250RR. It also gets a more premium Showa SFF-BP inverted front fork and a rear monoshock. Interestingly, apart from providing a more sophisticated damping, the front fork isn’t preload-adjustable like the telescopic fork in its predecessor.
